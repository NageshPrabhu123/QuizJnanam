Platform Features

Everything you need
for secure online exams

From question creation to result evaluation — QuizJnanam handles the full exam lifecycle with enterprise-grade security.

🤖
AI Face Detection
Real-time face monitoring detects no-face and looking-away events, instantly alerting the teacher with a camera snapshot.
🖥️
Screen Share Monitoring
Mandatory full-monitor screen sharing on desktop. Partial shares (tab or window) are rejected. Screenshots captured on every violation.
🔔
Real-time Violation Alerts
Teachers receive instant toast notifications with camera photos and screen captures the moment a student triggers any violation.
📝
MCQ, Descriptive & True/False
Create rich question papers with multiple question types, images, equations (KaTeX), and model answers. Auto-grade MCQ and T/F instantly.
⏱️
Scheduled Exams
Set precise start and end times for each exam. Students can only access the paper during the scheduled window. Auto-submits on timeout.
🎓
Section & Course Targeting
Assign exams to specific year, section (A/B/C) for 1st years or course branches (CSE, ECE, MECH etc.) for 2nd year onwards.
🚫
Zero-Tolerance Lockdown
Tab switches, Alt+Tab, DevTools, PrintScreen, paste — all blocked and logged. First fullscreen exit gives one warning, second = permanent ban. Teacher only can lift the ban.
📊
Instant Results Dashboard
Students see results immediately after submission. Teachers get a full results table with scores, time taken, pass/fail, and pending eval status, Students can take retest once ban is lifted.
✏️
Descriptive Evaluation
Teachers manually evaluate descriptive answers against model answers, assign marks, and the system recalculates the final score automatically.

How It Works

From setup to results
in minutes

1

Teacher Creates Exam

Add MCQ, descriptive, or true/false questions with images and equations. Set schedule, pass score, and target section.

2

Student Grants Permissions

Camera and full-screen share are granted before exam starts. Wrong share type is rejected with clear guidance.

3

Exam Runs in Lockdown

Fullscreen enforced. All navigation blocked. AI watches the camera. Any violation logs a screenshot and alerts teacher instantly.

4

Results & Evaluation

MCQ auto-graded instantly. Descriptive answers evaluated by teacher. Final score updated and visible to student immediately.

Security

Multi-layer protection
against cheating

Every possible cheating vector is monitored, logged, and flagged to the teacher in real time.

📸
Screenshot Detection
PrintScreen and Game Bar recording keys are blocked and trigger immediate ban.
📋
Paste Blocked
Copy, cut, and paste are completely disabled during the exam. Attempts are logged.
🔧
DevTools Banned
F12, Ctrl+Shift+I, and window size detection catch DevTools usage instantly.
😶
Face Monitoring
AI detects no face and looking-away every 1.5 seconds. Teacher alerted with photo.
🖥️
Full Monitor Required
Screen share must be the entire monitor. Tab or window shares are rejected.
🔀
Question Randomization
Each student gets questions in a unique order based on their ID — copying neighbours is futile.
🚫
Permanent Ban System
One serious violation = permanent ban from that exam. Stored in database. Cannot be bypassed. Teacher can Lift the ban at any time.
💻
VM Detection
Screen resolution mismatch flags potential virtual machine usage to the teacher.
